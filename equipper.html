App Guide & Equipper Features

Item Comparison

Hover over items in the inventory to see a live comparison against your currently equipped gear. Green values indicate improvements, while red indicates a stat decrease.

Presets & Storage

Save up to 8 unique equipment setups. Name your presets and use "Save" to store your current loadout (including gems) or "Load" to quickly switch back. Data is saved locally.

Gem Sockets

Neck and Ring slots support Gem Sockets. Equip a base item first, then select a gem from the available list to apply powerful percentage or flat stat multipliers.

Dynamic Item Display

Clicking an item (or a filled slot) updates the "Item Display" panel. This provides a permanent view of the item's stats, merchant value, and detailed drop locations.

Favourites System

Click the star (★) on any item to add it to your Favourites. These items stay pinned at the top of their respective categories and appear in the quick-access panel below.

Instant Equipping

You can instant equip items by pressing them in the favourites panel. This allows for rapid gear swaps without needing to navigate the main inventory categories.

Hunting Log

The Hunting Log automatically identifies which monsters/resources drop your currently equipped gear. It provides the enemy/resource name, level, and map location to help you track your progression sources.

Crafting Log

The Crafting Log displays the materials and requirements for equipped gear created at an Anvil. It also provides a "Total Materials" summary at the bottom, calculating exactly what you need to craft your full equipped set.
