Arena Operations

This arena is a high-speed training ground to master Winds of Valen combat logic. You must clear 5 consecutive rounds as fast as possible to register on the global leaderboard.

How it works:

  • Press the "Start" button to begin the clock.
  • Observe the Enemy's Attack and Defence.
  • Select the correct Shield to block their attack AND the correct Weapon to break their defence simultaneously.
  • Use Hotkeys (visible on buttons) for maximum speed. Selection is instant.
  • The clock pauses briefly between rounds for 1.2 seconds to allow you to reset.

Combat Counters Manual

  • Breaking Enemy Shields (Offense):
    • Enemy Block: Use Quick Damage, or Magic Damage
    • Enemy Evasion: Use Heavy Damage or Magic Damage
    • Enemy Warding: Use Heavy Damage, or Quick Damage
  • Countering Enemy Attacks (Defence):
    • Enemy Quick Damage: Use Evasion shield
    • Enemy Heavy Damage: Use Block shield
    • Enemy Magic Damage: Use Warding shield
